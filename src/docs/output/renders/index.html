Short Blurb:

"Analytics are made up of data that can be analyzed to draw logical conclusions, and for UX design specifically, that data contains information about the users of your app or website, such as their age, their location, their interests, or simply their behavior — that is, how they use your app or website."

Static 3D Renderings

These 3d renderings are produced by using CSS to wrap the full cover graphic produced above around a 3D shape. We're using PhantomJS to turn that HTML render into a set of flat PNGs. The 'camera angles' and aspects we choose are arbitary and there's nothing stopping us from 'posing' the book at any angle.
